Community Impact & Value

Soundry is dedicated to creating meaningful educational experiences in Omaha. Through experimental workshops, collaborative projects, and community engagement, we've worked with dozens of participants to explore sound, creativity, and collective learning. Our work demonstrates tangible impact on educational outcomes, community cohesion, and individual creative development.

Stories of Impact

"Soundry taught me that experimentation and failure are essential parts of learning. I gained confidence in my creative thinking and met people who shared my curiosity. This experience changed how I approach problem-solving in my career."

— Workshop Participant, Tech Professional

"As an educator, partnering with Soundry gave my students exposure to creative problem-solving and interdisciplinary thinking they don't get elsewhere. Students who were struggling in traditional settings thrived in this collaborative environment."

— Teacher, Omaha Schools

"Soundry created a space where I could be vulnerable, experimental, and supported. The community aspect was as important as the learning itself. I now apply these collaborative principles in my own work as an artist."

— Community Artist & Soundry Facilitator

"This initiative bridges gaps between schools, artists, and community members. It's exactly what Omaha needs—a model for experimental, community-driven education that honors creativity and critical thinking."

— Local Arts Organization Director
